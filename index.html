Bio

I'm a Postdoctoral Associate at the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems (LIDS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) working with Prof. Asu Ozdaglar and Prof. Ali Jadbabaie. My current research focuses on optimization for machine learning and deep learning theory. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara, in 2021, and my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles, in 2016. In 2019, I was a Finalist in the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship Program.
