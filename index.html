I'm a Postdoctoral Associate at the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems (LIDS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) fortunate to be working with Prof. Asu Ozdaglar and Prof. Ali Jadbabaie. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) in 2021, advised by Prof. Ramtin Pedarsani. Before that, I received my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in 2016. In 2019, I was a Finalist in the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship Program.

During the years, I have enjoyed collaborating closely with Prof. Aryan Mokhtari, Prof. Hamed Hassani, Prof. Farzan Farnia, and Prof. Salman Avestimehr.

My current research focuses on optimization for machine learning and deep learning theory.
